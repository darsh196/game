INSTRUCTIONS

  • To control the aircraft, use the up and down arrow keys.
  • There is a 1-minute timer. Make sure to intercept as many enemy aircrafts as possible!
  • You need to avoid colliding with enemy missiles or else you lose immediately.
  • Your score increases when you cross an enemy aircraft.
  • Be careful and become an ace pilot by achieving the top score!
